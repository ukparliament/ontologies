An instrument laid in draft before Parliament subject to either a negative, affirmative or super affirmative procedure. An instrument under the draft negative procedure may be made into law, if not objected to within the scrutiny period. Made statutory instrument workc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://parliament.uk/ontologies/delegation/MadeStatutoryInstrumentWork

is defined by
http://parliament.uk/ontologies/delegation/
Statutory instruments as signed into law. Made statutory instruments may result from a draft laid before Parliament, or may be laid as made, or may not be laid if the enabling legislation does not set out a duty to lay. Made statutory instruments laid before Parliament are subject to a negative, affirmative or super affirmative procedure, or none. An instrument under the made negative procedure remains law, unless objected to within the scrutiny period. An instrument under the made affirmative procedure or made super affirmative procedure remains law, if approved by Parliament within the approval period.
has super-classes

is in domain of

is in range of

Relates an enabled thing to the enabled thing it is replaced by. An enabled thing can be replaced by one enabled thing, or none. An enabled thing can replace one enabled thing, or none. Wholly revoked byop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: http://parliament.uk/ontologies/delegation/whollyRevokedBy

is defined by
http://parliament.uk/ontologies/delegation/
Relates a made statutory instrument to the made statutory instrument it is wholly revoked by. A made statutory instrument can be wholly revoked by one made statutory instrument, or none. A made statutory instrument can wholly revoke one or more made statutory instruments, or none. 'Wholly revoked by' is populated for made statutory instruments revoked whilst still before Parliament. Revoking secondary legislation has an effect similar to repealing primary legislation. Wholly revoking a made statutory instrument removes that made statutory instrument from the statute book on the date it is revoked.
has domain
Made statutory instrument workc
has range
Made statutory instrument workc
