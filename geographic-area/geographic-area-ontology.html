is defined by

Relates a boundary set to the statutory thing, or things, which established that boundary set. A boundary set may be established by one or more statutory things, for example: the 2010 boundary set in Wales being established by a single Order in Council and the 1955 boundary set in Wales being established by three Orders in Council. A statutory thing may establish one or more boundary sets, or none, for example: the Parliamentary Constituencies and Assembly Electoral Regions (Wales) Order 2006 establishing the 2010 boundary set in Wales and the Parliamentary Constituencies Order 2023 establishing boundary sets in England, Wales, Northern Ireland and Scotland.

Partly contained byop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: http://parliament.uk/ontologies/geographic-area/partlyContainedBy

is defined by
http://parliament.uk/ontologies/geographic-area/
Relates a geographic area to a geographic area it is partly contained by. A constituency area may be partly contained by a lower tier local authority area, by an upper tier local authority area, or by neither. A lower tier local authority area or an upper tier local authority area may be partly contained by a constituency area. The Local Democracy, Economic Development and Construction Act 2009 does not preclude an upper tier local authority area being partly contained by a combined authority area. As at November 2024, no upper tier local authority area is partly contained by a combined authority area.
has domain
Geographic areac
has range

Wholly contained byop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: http://parliament.uk/ontologies/geographic-area/whollyContainedBy

is defined by
http://parliament.uk/ontologies/geographic-area/
Relates a geographic area to the geographic area it is wholly contained by. A constituency area may be wholly contained by a lower tier local authority area, by an upper tier local authority area, or by neither. A constituency area in England is wholly contained by an English region. A constituency area is wholly contained by a country. A lower tier local authority area may be wholly contained by a constituency area. A lower tier local authority area is wholly contained by an upper tier local authority area, except where the lower tier local authority area is also the upper tier local authority area. An upper tier local authority area may be wholly contained by a constituency area. As at November 2024, an upper tier local authority area is wholly contained by a combined authority area, where a combined authority area exists. An upper tier local authority area in England is wholly contained by an English region. An upper tier local authority area is wholly contained by a country. A combined authority area may be wholly contained by an English region. A combined authority area is wholly contained by the country of England. An English region is wholly contained by the country of England.
has domain
Geographic areac
has range
Geographic areac
