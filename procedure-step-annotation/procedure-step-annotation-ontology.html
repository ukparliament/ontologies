A step of type business. A business step describes a set of potential business items, for example: the potential for papers to be laid before the House of Commons. Potential business items described by a business step may take place inside or outside Parliament.

is defined by
http://parliament.uk/ontologies/procedure-step-annotation/
A note describing the nature of the date that should be added to a business item actualising a business step. For example: a business item actualising a business step describing committee consideration should use the date that the committee met, rather than the date on which the committee report was published.
