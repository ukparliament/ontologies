Laying ontology

A model describing the laying of papers into a House and the authorities those papers are laid under.

Classes · Object properties · Data properties · Subclasses · Namespaces · Depiction

Classes

Class Laid paper

A paper having been laid before a House.

Superclasses · MadeAvailableThing · PaperExpression

Class Laying

The act of making a paper available to a House under a laying authority. Papers are made available to a House on being accepted by the Journal Office of that House.

Superclasses · MakingAvailable

Class Laying authority citation

A citation of the authority under which a paper is laid, for example: a section of legislation, a standing order or a resolution giving authorisation for that laying.

Class Laying authority type

The type of an authority under which a paper is laid. A paper may be laid under prerogative powers and said to be laid 'by command'. A paper laid in order to comply with legislation may be said to be laid 'by Act'. A paper may be laid in order to comply with standing orders, or with an opposed or unopposed return motion.

Class Made available thing

A thing made available to a House or to a House by means of its Library.

Class Making available

The act of making a thing available to a House or to a House by means of its Library.

Class Paper expression

A paper made available to a House, being an expression of a paper work.

Object properties

Object property Has laying authority citation

Relates an act of laying a paper to the citation of the authority under which that paper is laid. The laying of a paper has one authority citation, or none.

Laying — Has laying authority citation — LayingAuthorityCitation

Object property Has laying authority type

Relates a laying to the type of the authority under which that laying is made. A laying has one laying authority type.

Laying — Has laying authority type — LayingAuthorityType

Object property Parent laying authority type

Relates a laying authority type to the parent of that laying authority type, for example: a laying authority type of 'by Act' having a parent laying authority type of 'by Statute'. A laying authority type may have one parent laying authority type, or none. A laying authority type may have one or more child laying authority types, or none.

LayingAuthorityType — Parent laying authority type — LayingAuthorityType

Object property Results in

Relates an act of laying a paper to that paper having been laid. An act of laying is of one or more laid papers. A laid paper is the result of one act of laying.

Laying — Results in — LaidPaper
