Oral contribution ontology

A model describing the reporting of oral contributions to a parliamentary proceeding.

Classes · Object properties · Data properties · Subclasses · Namespaces · Depiction

Classes

Class Contributor presence

The presence of a contributor at a parliamentary proceeding, being either physically present or virtually present.

Class Credited oral contribution

A verbal contribution to a parliamentary proceeding made by an identified person, being incumbent in a position.

Superclasses · OralContribution

Class Credited oral contribution type

The type of a credited oral contribution, for example: an oral question, a maiden speech, a point of order, a Ministerial Statement or an intervention.

Class Event

An event, taking place in time and space.

Class Incumbency

The holding of a position by a person, including the time period for which this was the case. For example, Philip Hammond holding the position of Foreign Secretary between two dates.

Class Oral contribution

A verbal contribution to a parliamentary proceeding.

Superclasses · Event

Class Uncredited oral contribution

A verbal contribution to a parliamentary proceeding made by a person or persons who are not identified.

Superclasses · OralContribution

Object properties

Object property Contributed by

Relates a credited oral contribution to the incumbency - in a position - of the person identified as having made that contribution. A credited oral contribution is made by a person in one incumbency.

CreditedOralContribution — Contributed by — Incumbency

Object property Credited oral contribution type parent

Relates a credited oral contribution type to its parent type, for example: Ministerial Statements having a parent type of Oral Statements. A credited oral contribution type has one parent type, or none. A credited oral contribution type has one or more child types, or none.

CreditedOralContributionType — Credited oral contribution type parent — CreditedOralContributionType

Object property Has contributor presence

Relates a credited oral contribution to the presence of the person making that contribution. A credited oral contribution has one type of presence for the contributor.

CreditedOralContribution — Has contributor presence — ContributorPresence

Object property Has credited oral contribution type

Relates an oral contribution to its type. An oral contribution has one or more types. For example: an oral contribution being both an intervention and on a point of order.

CreditedOralContribution — Has credited oral contribution type — CreditedOralContributionType

Object property In reply to

Relates a credited oral contribution to the credited oral contribution it is in reply to, for example: an answer being in reply to a question. A credited oral contribution may be a reply to one credited oral contribution. A credited oral contribution may have one credited oral contribution in reply.

CreditedOralContribution — In reply to — CreditedOralContribution

Object property Resumes from

Relates a credited oral contribution to the credited oral contribution it resumes from, for example: an answer resuming following an intervention. A credited oral contribution may resume from one credited oral contribution. A credited oral contribution may be resumed by one credited oral contribution.

CreditedOralContribution — Resumes from — CreditedOralContribution
